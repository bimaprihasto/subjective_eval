This webpage are experimental results on 3/28, In this experiment, which is a lightweight model, I conducted training with 20 voice speech data on each speaker, so that there are 40 voice speech data in each pair. The data is non-parallel, I did an experiment with the CVC [1] and CycleGAN [2] methods. Using VCTK corpus [3] which has a total of 44 hours of voice speech from 109 speakers.
